Harmful Meme Detection System

Upload a meme image to analyse harmful intent with an ExplainHM-style pipeline. The system now shows two AI viewpoints (DeepSeek vs Aliyun) and then produces a final explainable decision.

Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, WebP (max 16 MB)

How It Works

🖼️ Multimodal Extraction

The system extracts meme text (OCR / vision), plus image features such as contrast, brightness, and text-region signals.

🟦 DeepSeek View (Benign Side)

DeepSeek generates a rationale from the "not harmful" perspective, explaining why the meme may be interpreted safely.

🟥 Aliyun View (Harmful Side)

Aliyun vision model generates a rationale from the "harmful" perspective, focusing on implicit hate, discrimination, or misinformation cues.

⚖️ Final Decision & Explanation

A final judge combines both AI rationales (and optional small-model refinement) to output the final label and transparent explanation.
